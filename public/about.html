About OpenSpots

We believe parking should be simple, smart, and sustainable. OpenSpots was built to end the frustration of circling lots, guessing availability, and wasting time. With our sensor-powered system and seamless app integration, we’re creating a future where finding a parking spot is effortless.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make parking effortless for drivers and profitable for property owners — while helping cities reduce congestion and emissions. We combine IoT sensors, AI, and data-driven insights to create smarter urban mobility.

Contact Our Team

Driving a Greener Future

Every minute spent searching for a parking spot contributes to unnecessary CO₂ emissions. OpenSpots helps reduce pollution and congestion by guiding drivers directly to open or reserved spots in real time. By connecting smart sensors, predictive analytics, and user data, we’re building an ecosystem where convenience and sustainability go hand in hand — whether you’re reserving event parking or finding the closest available city space.

How It Works

OpenSpots uses a combination of AI and IoT sensor data to monitor parking availability in real time. Every sensor communicates directly with our cloud platform, providing instant feedback to your app or browser. This ensures you always receive accurate information on open spots and verified reservations. By eliminating guesswork, OpenSpots keeps your experience safe, smooth, and dependable from the moment you arrive to the moment you leave.
